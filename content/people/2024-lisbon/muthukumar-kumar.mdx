Muthukumar Kumar, Co-founder and Managing Editor at Geoawesome, is a technology evangelist, strategic advisor, and partnership architect with over a decade of experience in the tech industry. He collaborates with industry leaders, academia, and government organizations to drive innovation and build strategic partnerships. His mission is to advocate for the transformative potential of geospatial data and technologies, creating data-driven solutions to tackle critical global challenges.
