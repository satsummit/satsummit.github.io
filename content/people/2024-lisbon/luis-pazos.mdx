Luis Pazos is a Staff Researcher at Degas, where he leads the development of a foundation model centered on agriculture. Previously, he was a Senior Researcher at Google, where he spearheaded AI initiatives in the agricultural sector, leveraging remote sensing to monitor farming activities in Brazil and the U.S. Before joining Google, he conducted electrical engineering research as a Heising-Simons Research Fellow at UC Berkeley. Luis holds a Ph.D. in physics from the University of Cambridge (2016), and his work has been published in top-tier journals, including those of the National Academy of Sciences, the Nature group, and Science.
