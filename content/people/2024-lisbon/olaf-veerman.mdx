Olaf leads project operations at Development Seed, an engineering company that is accelerating the application of earth data to our biggest global challenges. We leverage massive earth data, cloud computing, geospatial AI, and thoughtful product development to provide data scientists and decisionmakers with a clear view of a rapidly changing planet.

For over 20 years, Olaf has supported organizations to create positive social impact through the use of technology and data. Olaf was a founding board member of OpenAQ. Prior to joining Development Seed, he founded and ran the Lisbon based technology firm Flipside.
