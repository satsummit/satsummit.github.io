Alyssa is a Product Strategist at the World Resources Institute. She develops and implements strategies to deliver geospatial data from Land & Carbon Lab to support nature protection and restoration and to ensure widespread access to data. Previously at WRI, Alyssa led development of the Global Forest Watch platform for 8 years.
