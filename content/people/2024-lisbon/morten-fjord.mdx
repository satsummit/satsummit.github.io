Morten Fjord is a co-founder of ClearSKY Vision, a company advancing earth observation through increased temporal resolution using artificial intelligence. He holds a Master’s degree in Management Accounting and Information Systems with a specialization in social data science. Over the past five years, Morten has worked extensively with optical earth observation data across various applications, including continuous monitoring, vegetation mapping, variable-rate fertilizer deployment, and near real-time data acquisition, among others. His work integrates technical and strategic insights, supporting ClearSKY Vision’s mission to make actionable, high-frequency earth observation data accessible to decision-makers worldwide.
